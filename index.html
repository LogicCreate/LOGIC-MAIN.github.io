About

LogicPad is a low-code application development platform that simplifies the process of building software applications on the Windows operating system.
It provides an intuitive interface and drag-and-drop functionality, allowing users to create applications without extensive programming knowledge.
LogicPad was born from over two decades of experience in C++ development, with the goal of turning accumulated technical knowledge into something meaningful and accessible to everyone.
Whether you're a student just beginning to learn programming, a business professional aiming to streamline daily workflows, or an engineer involved in digital transformation, LogicPad is designed to empower you.
What sets LogicPad apart is its AI-friendly architecture, enabling seamless integration with modern AI tools and workflows.
Additionally, the platform supports 97 languages, making it accessible to users all around the world.
Most importantly, it is completely free to use, in the spirit of leaving no one behind.
